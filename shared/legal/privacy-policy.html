Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2025

Just Spent is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our voice-enabled expense tracking application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Expense Data: Amount, currency, category, merchant name, notes, and transaction dates
  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings when you use voice commands to log expenses
  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and user preferences
  • Currency Preferences: Default currency and multi-currency settings
  • Budget Information: Monthly budgets and category-specific budget limits

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Data: Feature usage patterns, app interactions, and performance metrics
  • Location Data: Approximate location for transaction context (optional, with your permission)
  • Technical Data: IP address, crash reports, and diagnostic information

1.3 Voice Data

When you use voice commands via Siri (iOS) or Google Assistant (Android):

  • Voice recordings are processed locally on your device whenever possible
  • Voice transcripts are temporarily stored for 7 days to improve accuracy
  • No voice recordings are shared with third parties
  • You can disable voice features at any time in app settings

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Purposes

  • Expense Tracking: Store and organize your financial transactions
  • Voice Processing: Convert voice commands to expense entries
  • Data Synchronization: Sync your data across devices (if enabled)
  • Analytics: Generate spending insights and summaries
  • Budget Management: Track spending against your defined budgets

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Improve voice recognition accuracy
  • Enhance category classification algorithms
  • Optimize app performance and fix bugs
  • Develop new features based on usage patterns

2.3 Communications

  • Send important app updates and security notices
  • Provide budget alerts and spending reminders (if enabled)
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Just Spent uses a local-first architecture:

  • All expense data is stored locally on your device
  • iOS: Core Data with encryption
  • Android: Room database with encryption
  • No cloud storage required for basic functionality

3.2 Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable cloud synchronization:

  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256)
  • Stored securely in compliance with industry standards
  • You can disable sync and delete cloud data at any time

3.3 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Authentication: Biometric (Face ID/Touch ID/Fingerprint) or PIN protection
  • Access Control: App-level security with auto-lock timeout
  • Secure APIs: All network communications use HTTPS
  • No Third-Party Access: Your financial data is never sold or shared with advertisers

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

Just Spent does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics tools (anonymized data only)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing

4.3 Third-Party Services

Just Spent integrates with:

  • Apple Siri (iOS): Voice processing follows Apple's privacy policy
  • Google Assistant (Android): Voice processing follows Google's privacy policy
  • Analytics Tools: Anonymized usage data for app improvement

5. Voice Data Privacy

5.1 Processing

  • Voice commands are processed on-device whenever possible
  • Siri/Google Assistant may process voice on their servers per their privacy policies
  • Just Spent only receives the transcribed text, not audio recordings

5.2 Retention

  • Voice transcripts stored for maximum 7 days for accuracy improvement
  • Automatically deleted after retention period
  • You can manually delete transcripts in app settings

5.3 Control

  • Disable voice features at any time
  • Revoke microphone permissions in device settings
  • Use manual entry exclusively if preferred

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your stored data within the app
  • Export: Download your data in portable format (CSV, JSON)
  • Edit: Modify or correct your expense data
  • Delete: Remove individual expenses or all data
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics, voice features, or cloud sync

6.2 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

Under GDPR, you also have:

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to be forgotten (complete data deletion)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to automated decision-making

6.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)

Under CCPA, you have:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

7. Data Retention

  • Expense Data: Retained indefinitely until you delete it
  • Voice Transcripts: Automatically deleted after 7 days
  • Deleted Items: Permanently removed after 30-day recovery period
  • Account Data: Deleted within 90 days of account closure request
  • Backups: Retained for 90 days, then permanently deleted

8. Children's Privacy

Just Spent is not intended for users under 13 years old (16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

If you use Just Spent outside your home country:

  • Data may be transferred to and processed in other countries
  • We ensure adequate protections through standard contractual clauses
  • Your data rights remain protected regardless of location

10. Cookies and Tracking

Just Spent mobile apps do not use cookies. We may collect:

  • Anonymous analytics data (device type, OS version, app version)
  • Crash reports for debugging (no personal information)
  • Feature usage statistics (aggregated and anonymized)

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be:

  • Posted in the app with "Last Updated" date
  • Notified via in-app message for material changes
  • Effective immediately upon posting (or as stated)

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Open Source

Just Spent is an open-source project. You can:

  • Review our source code on GitHub: github.com/maneesh888/just-spent
  • Verify our privacy claims through code inspection
  • Contribute to improving privacy features
  • Self-host if you prefer complete control

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@justspent.app
  • GitHub Issues: github.com/maneesh888/just-spent/issues
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 7 business days

For data deletion requests, please use the in-app "Delete My Data" option in Settings for immediate processing.
